About Us

EcoWhite Lab is a pioneering force in white pollution solutions. Using advanced technology, we develop biodegradable materials and innovative recycling systems to replace traditional plastics. Our mission is to reduce plastic waste and promote a circular economy. We collaborate with businesses, communities, and governments to implement sustainable practices, aiming to protect our oceans and ecosystems. Committed to environmental stewardship, we strive to create a cleaner, greener future free from plastic pollution.
